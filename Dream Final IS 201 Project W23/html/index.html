My Favorite Hobbies/Interests

Growing up in Thailand, food was a huge part of our culture. I have always loved cooking since I was younger. My mom has taught me how to cook without recipes. Surprisingly, her food will taste just right every time! After moving to the States, food brings back a strong sense of nostalgia and comfort. The fragrance of Pad Thai often reminds me of sitting with my family eating our favorite dish together as a family.

High Fitness

When I was younger, I used to hate exercising and sports. As I explore other exercises, I realized that running is not the only sports. I learned that I like to exercise with music and dance. My friends invited me to do High Fitness with them, and I fell in love. High Fitness was very hard at first. But, I have gotten used to it more and it has brought me a lot of joy and satisfaction.

Traveling Around the World

Traveling was one of the things that my family did together when I was younger. I love getting to be a part of the different side of the world. Getting to see people, food, and culture of all around the world was an eye-opening experiences. People are so different and unique in each country.
